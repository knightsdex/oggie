The frog that started it all

Oggie the Frog, created by underground comic legend Robert Crumb in the 1960s, is the original frog that inspired generations of quirky art and characters. His iconic debut in The Yum Yum Book laid the groundwork for later cultural icons, including Matt Furie’s Pepe the Frog. Oggie’s satirical, countercultural roots made him a symbol of rebellion and creativity. Now, with his transition to Ethereum, Oggie continues to inspire, bringing together art and blockchain technology for a modern audience while honoring his place as the true original frog.
